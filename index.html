Places You Should Visit!

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower in Paris, France, and one of the most recognizable structures in the world.

The Statue of Liberty is a colossal neoclassical sculpture on Liberty Island, New York City, symbolizing freedom and democracy.

The Colosseum is an ancient amphitheater in Rome, Italy, known for its iconic architecture and historical significance.

The Great Wall of China is a historic series of walls and fortifications in China, built to protect against invasions and raids.
